## The problem
Golf instruction is locked to the coach's language. A learner who thinks in another language gets the lesson second-hand, through translation, losing the nuance that makes it land.

## The build
Birdie is the same SeedWave engine that powers Treino — pointed at a completely different domain. That's the whole point: **one engine, many products.** Multilingual, built on the three-layer architecture `engine / locales / content`, where the lesson content is just another layer on top of a proven core.

## Stack
`SeedWave three-layer engine` · `multilingual (RU/EN/PT/ES/DE)` · `GitHub Pages` · `two-theme UI (day/night)`
